Our Tours

Alicante

Famous for the explanada this city has both beaches and architecture and won't disapoint those looking for a party either.

Ronda & Granada

Two amazing cities in Andalucia Ronda boasts one of the most extraordinary locations in Spain, sitting as it does each side of the 100 metre (328 foot) deep El Tajo gorge. There is some wonderful walking to be enjoyed in this spectacular cleft in the landscape: from the old town, a dramatic path takes you right down to the bottom of El Tajo, to the foot of the mighty “New Bridge” that spans the canyon.

Benidorm

A city in the Comunidad Valenciana Famous for art, museums, great nightlife, and of course beaches. Just a short train ride from either Barcelona or Alicante. Great for those who love beaches and beer. This city is commonly known as the city in Spain with the cheapest beer.

Baqueira

The ultimate destination for Skiing and Snowboarding in Spain. Filled with Tourists from France, Portugal and the Nordic countries, this place won't let you down if you like winter sports.

Burgos & Valladolid

These are 2 amazing cities in Castilla y Leon Perfect for foodies, families, and those seeking a less stressful vacation while still seeing a very authentic Spain.

Bilbao & San Sebastian

The ultimate destination for foodies in Spain. With more Michelin star restaurants per capita than anywhere in Europe, for those who love gastronomic tourism this place will not le you down.

Canary Islands - Gran Canaria

As a new hub for digital nomads and crypto enthusiasts the beaches of the Canary Islands are absolutely spectacular almost any time of the year.

Canary Islands - Fuerteventura

Typically known as an island for adventure seekers this is a ultimate location for those who love hiking, windsurfing and a true rustic experience.

Classic & Modern Barcelona

Arguably Spain's most iconic city art, architecture, and food lovers will be delighted with everything that this city has to offer. With amazing train connections to France, this is the ultimate jumping off point for those looking to head to France as part of their vacation.

Denia, Xavia, Altea

These three coastal cities in the Comunidad Valenciana are perfect for families looking to enjoy the beach in a relaxed atmosphere. Just a short train ride from Alicante or Valencia.

Girona Castle

The ultimate tour for Game of Thrones fans. Most of the scenes for this series were filmed in Girona.

Historic Madrid

This bustling city is not for the faint hearted. The capital of Spain won't disappoint. Imagine a city where at 10am on the metro on Saturdays its nearly impossible to tell who is starting their day and who is finishing their night. This bustling capital is great for everything except relaxing.

Ibiza Party Vacation

Known as the party island of the world this is the perfect place to spend $1,000 for a bottle in a nightclub or daytime beach club or and rent a boat for a daytime party. While it is possible to do a family vacation here, this is generally known as the party place and is best to go with friends.

Leon

An amazing city of 100,000 inhabitants this is a perfect city for gastronomic tourism, architecture, and a relaxing, walkable vacation. A good place hang your hat for some time after or before seeing Madrid.

Sevilla

The home of flamenco the main industry here is tourism. Plenty of sangria, art, and of course British tourists.

Malaga & Puerto Banus

A perfect beach destination for those looking for a more luxurious vacation experience.

Marbella

One of the most beautiful cities on the costa del sol boating is the main attraction here. If you don't own a yacht or sailboat, you can rent one.

Valencia

Famous for museums and partying Valencia has great train connections to Alicante and Barcelona. It is great for families and single travelers alike. While it boasts a beach, the cultural attractions here are what it's most famous for.
